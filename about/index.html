Welcome to Cloudnative.nz- Building Together in Open Source

We are dedicated to fostering collaboration and empowering individuals to make a positive impact through open source contributions. Our mission goes beyond code; it's about working together to support our communities and create opportunities for everyone.

Open source is not just about software development; it's a philosophy that encourages transparency, cooperation, and the sharing of knowledge. By joining the open source movement, you have the chance to be a part of something bigger, to shape the future of technology, and to give back to the global community.

Whether you are an experienced developer or new to the world of coding, there are countless ways to get involved and make a difference. Explore the opportunities, mentorships, training programs, and community engagement initiatives below to start your journey in open source.

Remember, open source is not just about coding; it's about improving documentation, making suggestions, contributing artwork, and much more. Everyone can play a role in this collaborative ecosystem.

What is the cloud?

Cloud computing is the delivery of various services through the internet. These resources are stored on servers in data centers across the globe.

  • Resources are components or microservices that make an application.
  • When you access a website that is a cloud native application, a request is made to the cloud server.
  • The resources are then compiled and sent back to your device. This means that very little of your own hardware is used to run and display the application as the application is running on a remote cloud server.

How cloud technology impacts you

Cloud native technology has positively transformed our digital world, enabling faster load times, seamless data synchronization across devices, and effortless information sharing.

While proprietary software has its strengths, it is closed-source, restricting users' access to its code, and limiting modifications and distribution.

Embracing open-source solutions fosters innovation, data security, and the collective betterment of technology for everyone, empowering a brighter digital future.

What is Open Source?

Open Source is computer code all users can inspect, modify and enhance. Because it’s ‘open’, you can own it, create, and innovate.

At its core, open source embraces and celebrates principles of open exchange, collaboration, transparency and community-oriented development.

The result is more than just tech, but a unique way of interacting with one another to innovate solutions and shape the world around us for the better.

Unlocking Possibilities: Embrace Open Source and Cloud Native Technology


Open source + Cloud native technology allows for more inclusive and trusted infrastructure, by understanding the core mechanics of software engineering and cloud infrastructure you can use these tools with confidence and understanding, whilst contributing to a global movement that effects our daily lives.

There are still large problems that need solving with the technology we consume and create and we need keen and inspired minds to join us on this journey.
